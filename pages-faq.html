F.A.Q.

When will my credit card be charged the annual membership fees using automatic renewal?

Your primary credit card on file will be charged the relevant membership(s) and subscription(s) fees the second week of October each year, i.e. the week beginning with the second Sunday of the month. If you do not wish to proceed with automatic renewal for an upcoming membership year, you must opt out before the second week of October through your member profile in "My Account". The link to sign in is located at the top of the IEEE page. Please refer to the terms and conditions for details.

What can I access with my IEEE Account?

IEEE Account has been enhanced to significantly improve the overall IEEE experience, making it a simpler, easier and faster process. The link to sign in using your IEEE Account can be located at the top of any IEEE page. Visit "My Account" and sign in using your IEEE Account to: · Manage your IEEE Membership · Personalize your IEEE Experience · Get Member Discounts · Manage your IEEE Purchase History · Navigate to a different IEEE website without having to re-authenticate · Add items from multiple IEEE websites and checkout with a single step Using your IEEE Account you can also access: · IEEE Xplore Digital Library · IEEE Standards Association · IEEE Spectrum Online · MyIEEE · EWH (1.0 & 2.0) · vTools · Online Senior Member Application

How do I change my IEEE Account information?

Once you have signed in with your IEEE Account, you will notice that your name will appear on the top right corner. Place your mouse over your name to display a drop down menu of options for managing your account. The link to sign in is located at the top of the IEEE page. You will be able to update and view the following information: · IEEE Account · Password · Communication Preferences · Personal Profile · Professional and Education Information · Your IEEE Purchase Information Once your selection has been updated, click on "Save" to finalize the change to your account.

How do I renew my annual membership?

You can renew your annual membership and customize your subscription options through the IEEE Membership and Subscriptions catalog. You can sign into your IEEE Account using your login credentials, the link is located at the top of any IEEE page. When you renew online your receipt will be presented on the screen for you to print. It provides a summary of the information you submitted. Print this page and save it for your records. This will be your official receipt for your annual membership renewal. In addition, you will receive an email order confirmation.

I failed to renew my membership and it has been more than 90 days. How can I access my GoogleApps@IEEE account?

IEEE Memberships not renewed in a timely manner (less the 90 days from when your dues are considered past due), had their GoogleApps@IEEE accounts and all the contents (mail, contacts, calendar) deleted. If you renewed your membership after 90 days since your lapse in membership, you can create a new GoogleApps@IEEE account.
